Lohri Melodies
Heartbeat of Punjab

Songs of Fire & Folklore

Lohri songs serve as a traditional record of the past, honoring the tale of Dulla Bhatti and commemorating unity, courage, and appreciation with poignant lyrics.

More Than Just Melodies

Sung during bonfire gatherings or while going door-to-door for Lohri, these songs serve as a bridge between generations, imparting lessons of bravery, kindness, and belief.

History

Honoring folk heroes like Dulla Bhatti who fought for justice.

Harvest

Expressing gratitude to nature for fertility and abundance.

Blessings

Showering love and good wishes on newborns and newlyweds.

Legendary Verses

The Most Famous Songs

1. Sundar Mundariye Ho

ਸੁੰਦਰ ਮੁੰਦਰਿਏ ਹੋ!
ਤੇਰਾ ਕੌਣ ਵਿਚਾਰਾ ਹੋ?
ਦੁੱਲਾ ਭੱਟੀ ਵਾਲਾ ਹੋ!
ਦੁੱਲੇ ਦੀ ਧੀ ਵਿਆਹੀ ਹੋ!

Translation:

'O lovely Sundri! Who will look after you? Dulla Bhatti is your guardian! He wedded his daughter with pride...'

Significance: Recognizes Dulla Bhatti's efforts in safeguarding orphan girls, and calls for blessings for protection and promoting social responsibility.

2. Dulla Bhatti Ho

ਦੁੱਲਾ ਭੱਟੀ ਹੋ!
ਸ਼ੇਰ ਪੰਜਾਬ ਦਾ ਹੋ!
ਧਨੀਆਂ ਨੂੰ ਲੁੱਟਿਆ ਹੋ!
ਗਰੀਬਾਂ ਨੂੰ ਦਿੱਤਾ ਹੋ!

Translation:

O Dulla Bhatti! Brave warrior of Punjab! He took from the wealthy and shared with the needy!

Significance: Honors the fight against tyranny. Preserves the bravery and fairness depicted in Punjabi tales.

3. Ho Tera Kaun Vichara Ho

ਹੋ ਤੇਰਾ ਕੌਣ ਵਿਚਾਰਾ ਹੋ?
ਅੰਬਰਾਂ ਤੋਂ ਉਤਰਿਆ ਹੋ!
ਰੱਬ ਨੇ ਭੇਜਿਆ ਹੋ!

Meaning:

'Who will shield you? An angel sent from above, God has bestowed a protector.'

Significance: Shows belief in divine protection and optimism, particularly crucial in severe winters when survival relied on nature.

Nature & Play

Gratitude & Joy

Harvest Gratitude

ਆਦਿ ਆਉਣੀ ਲੋਹੜੀ ਵੇ,
ਵੇਖੋ ਜੱਟਾਂ ਦੀ ਜੋੜੀ ਵੇ...

Lohri is here, Witness the solidarity of the farmers, Give sesame, indulge in sweets...

Expresses hope for prosperity and thanks nature for the harvest.

Children's Collection

ਲੋਹੜੀ ਆਈ, ਲੋਹੜੀ ਆਈ,
ਜੀਵਣ ਜਗਾਇਆ, ਫਸਲ ਲਿਆਈ...

Lohri is here, Celebrating life and abundance, Share money, sesame seeds, and sweets, Else the fire will play tricks on you!

Lighthearted verses promoting sharing, generosity, and festive fun.

Songs for New Beginnings

Linking Lohri to fertility, family legacy, and the joy of existence.

ਪੁੱਤ ਜੰਮਿਆ ਘਰ ਖੁਸ਼ੀ ਆਈ,
ਲੋਹੜੀ ਦੀ ਅੱਗ ਰੌਸ਼ਨਾਈ
ਲੰਮੀ ਉਮਰ, ਚੜ੍ਹਦੀ ਕਲਾ,
ਵੱਸਦਾ ਰਹੇ ਇਹ ਘਰ ਸਦਾ

With the birth of a son, joy fills the household, The Lohri fire glows brightly, Wishing for a flourishing life and high spirits, May prosperity always reign in this home.
