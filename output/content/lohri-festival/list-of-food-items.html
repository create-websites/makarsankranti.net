The Essence of the Meal

A thoughtfully curated Lohri menu goes beyond mere food - it embodies a spirit of warmth, generosity, and unity. Each dish, from traditional offerings to beloved favorites, narrates its own tale.

Ritual First

Traditional offerings such as Rewari and Peanuts are presented to the bonfire before being shared.

Hosting Tips for Lohri

Keep it Warm

Ensure food stays warm. Use insulated casseroles or food warmers.

Seasonal Ingredients

Highlight winter staples like jaggery, sesame, and maize.

Balance Flavors

Pair elaborate entrees (such as Dal Makhani) with uncomplicated accompaniments like salad.

Self-Serve

Encourage self-serve counters for ease during larger gatherings.
