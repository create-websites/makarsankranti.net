Lohri for Kids
Ages 4–12 Years

Fun, Learning & Joy

Experience the essence of joy, warmth, and togetherness with our interactive checklist for kids to make Lohri unforgettable with crafts, stories, and play.

Connecting Kids to Culture

This guide emphasizes enjoyable activities that educate tradition through hands-on experiences. From secure bonfires to tasty treats, each activity fosters cultural understanding and imagination.

Be Creative

Participate in creative activities such as crafting paper bonfires or designing greeting cards.

Move & Groove

Learn traditional songs and simple Bhangra steps to build confidence.

Story Time

Engage with your heritage by sharing stories like Dulla Bhatti and your family's past.
