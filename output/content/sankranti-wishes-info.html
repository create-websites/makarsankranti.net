Known by many names across India, this festival marks the sun's transition into Capricorn. It's a time of celebration, gratitude for the harvest, and vibrant cultural expressions that unite the nation in spirit.

One Nation, Many Names

From the kite-filled skies of the west to the sweet rice preparations of the south, each region adds its unique flavor to the celebration. Here's a glimpse of the festival's diverse identity across India.

A Symphony of Greetings

The joy of the festival is echoed in the diverse languages of India. Each greeting, unique in its script and sound, carries the same warm wishes for prosperity and happiness.

Hindi

मकर संक्रांति की शुभकामनाएँ!

"Makar Sankranti ki shubhkamnayein!"

Tamil

பொங்கல் வாழ்த்துக்கள்!

"Pongal Vazhthukkal!"

Gujarati

ઉત્તરાયણની શુભકામનાઓ!

"Uttarayan ni shubhkamnao!"

Telugu

సంక్రాంతి శుభాకాంక్షలు!

"Sankranti Subhakankshalu!"

Punjabi

ਲੋਹੜੀ ਦੀਆਂ ਲੱਖ ਲੱਖ ਵਧਾਈਆਂ!

"Lohri diyan lakh lakh vadhaiyan!"

Marathi

तिळगुळ घ्या, गोड गोड बोला!

"Tilgul ghya, god god bola!"
